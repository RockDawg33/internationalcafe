International Cafe is a simple place to share travel experiences, thoughtful recommendations, and practical tips with a spirit of care — for local communities, for small businesses, and for the places we visit. I’m starting with my own family’s experiences and hope, over time, to grow this into a welcoming archive for conscientious travelers everywhere.

Useful Tips

  • Choose local ferries, cafes, and family-run restaurants when possible.
  • Bring reusable water bottles and small snacks to reduce waste.
  • Travel at a slower pace and leave room for unplanned discoveries.
  • Look for places where walking, public transit, or ferries reduce the need for cars.
